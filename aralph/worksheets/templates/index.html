OUR FACULTY

Ang staff ng Aral Tayo!

ABI TAN

Materials and Resources
Department

Teacher Abi is a graduate of Library and Information Science from UP Diliman. She taught English in high school for a year but soon discovered that it was so difficult working with older kids particularly if they spent years not being motivated in school. She then decided to teach in preschool. She worked at Little Archers for five years before switcing to private tutoring to be able to spend more time with her "little man". She has been working as a private tutor slash playmate slash yaya to her "babies" for nearly five years now. During that time, she has accrued a cabinetful of materials which she thinks will benefit other students. This is the reason why she decided to create teacherabiworksheets. Plus, posting the worksheets online makes it easier for her to retrieve materials in case she forgets to print out the ones that she needs to use for that day.
